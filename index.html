How it works
Up and running in an afternoon

We handle the setup. You get answers to questions you've never been able to ask before.

1

We set it up on your server

EBMS Live installs directly on your Windows server alongside EBMS. It connects read-only to your Postgres database. No changes to EBMS, no data leaves your building.

2

Instant access from any browser

Your team accesses the dashboard from any browser, anywhere. No VPN required. A secure Cloudflare tunnel handles the connection.

3

Ask questions in plain English

Type any business question and get a direct answer from your live EBMS data. Sales, AR, inventory, purchasing, and anything else in your database.

Features
Built for EBMS businesses

Live KPI Dashboard

Revenue, AR aging, open invoices, and top customers. Always up to date, direct from your EBMS database. No exports, no stale spreadsheets.

Natural Language Queries

Ask any business question in plain English. The AI translates it to SQL, runs it against your live data, and gives you a plain-English answer.

Revenue Charts

Visual sales trends, month-over-month comparisons, and top-line charts that update in real time as orders come through EBMS.

Secure by Design

Read-only database access. Runs on your server. No EBMS data is ever sent to a third party. Bearer token auth and rate limiting included.

Access from Anywhere

Browser-based, mobile-friendly. No VPN, no remote desktop. Your sales rep can check AR from their phone on the road.

Automatic Updates

New features and improvements roll out automatically. Your server pulls updates from our stable branch every 6 hours. No manual installs.
