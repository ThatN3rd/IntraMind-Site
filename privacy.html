Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 9, 2025

Introduction

IntraMind is operated by Marc Dyck and is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our service.

We believe in transparency and keeping things simple. This policy describes our current data practices and will be updated as our service evolves.

Information We Collect

1. Account Request Information

When you submit an account request through our signup form, we collect:

  • Full Name - To identify you and personalize your experience
  • Email Address - To create your account and communicate with you
  • Company Name - To understand your organizational context

This information is temporarily stored only for the purpose of creating your account and is destroyed once your account has been created.

2. Account Credentials

Once your account is created, we store:

  • Email Address - Used as your login identifier
  • Password - Stored securely using industry-standard hashing techniques

3. Chat Conversations

When you use the IntraMind AI assistant:

  • Chat Messages - Your questions and our responses are stored in our database to provide context and memory for ongoing conversations
  • Conversation History - Allows the AI to understand the context of your questions and provide better assistance over time

4. Third-Party Processing

Your chat messages are processed by OpenAI's API to generate responses. OpenAI handles this data according to their own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing OpenAI's Privacy Policy to understand how they process data.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • Account Creation - To set up and manage your IntraMind account
  • Service Delivery - To provide you with AI assistance and EBMS support
  • Conversation Memory - To maintain context across your chat sessions and improve the quality of responses
  • Authentication - To verify your identity and secure your account

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any other purposes beyond providing the IntraMind service.

Data Storage and Security

Current Storage

Your data is currently stored in a local SQLite database. We are in the process of migrating to PostgreSQL for improved security and reliability.

Security Measures

  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard cryptographic techniques
  • Database access is restricted and secured
  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect against unauthorized access

Data Retention

  • Signup Information - Destroyed after account creation is complete
  • Account Credentials - Retained for as long as your account is active
  • Chat History - Retained to provide ongoing conversation context and improve your experience

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

OpenAI API

Your chat messages are sent to OpenAI's API to generate AI responses. OpenAI processes this data according to their privacy policy and API terms of service. OpenAI does not use API data to train their models.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or other legal process, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights or the safety of others.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access - You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction - You can request that we correct any inaccurate information
  • Deletion - You can request that we delete your account and associated data
  • Data Portability - You can request a copy of your data in a structured format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Cookies and Tracking

We do not currently use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies on our website.

Your browser may use session cookies for basic functionality, but we do not explicitly set or track cookies at this time. If this changes in the future, we will update this policy and provide notice.

International Users

IntraMind primarily serves users in Canada and the United States. Your data is stored and processed in accordance with Canadian privacy laws.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as our service evolves. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
